Soft Oatmeal Cookies

Description

Cookies that Grandma would make, except it's better! These soft and sweet cookies are easy to make and even easier to eat!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1.5 teaspoons fround cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup unsalted butter,softened
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 4 cups quick cooking oats
  • nonstick cooking spray with flour
  • 2 tablespoons water
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar, or as needed

Steps

  1. Whisk flour, cinnamon, baking soda, and salt together in a medium bowl until well combined.
  2. Beat butter, 1 cup white sugar, and brown sugar in a large bowl with an electric mixer until creamy, at least 2 to 3 minutes.Beat in eggs, one at time,then mix in vanilla. Gradually mix in dry ingredients until well combined. Add oats and mix thoroughly incorporated. Cover the bowl and chill dough in the refrigerator for at least 1 hour.
  3. When ready to bake, preheat the oven to 375 degrees F. Spray two cookie sheets with floured cooking spray. Place water in a small bowl and 2 tablespoons sugar in another small bowl.
  4. Roll chilled dough into walnut-sized balls, and place 2 inches apart on the prepared cookie sheets, Dip a large fork in water, then in sugar, and use to flatten each cookie.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven until light golden brown around the edges and centers are nearly set,8 to 10 minutes, switching racks halfway through. Allow cookies to cool on baking sheet for 5 minutes before transferring to a wire rack to cool completely.

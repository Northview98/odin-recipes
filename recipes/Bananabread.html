Banana chocolate chip bread

Description

You don't have to be a monkey, to love this recipe! This Banana bread recipe combines two awesome main ingredients: bananas and chocolate chips! Fun for a nice dessert at dinner; or whenever you want to release your inner chimp!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 3 ripe bananas, mashed
  • 1 tablespoon milk
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter,softened
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup semisweet chocolate chips
